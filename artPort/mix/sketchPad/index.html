Sketchpad Manifesto

The Sketchpad Manifesto originallys started in November of 2017 as an experiment to exercise and record my sketches while riding public transportation. Within a few weeks the idea had evolved into a year long project that provided an outlet for artistic development and reflection. Since starting the blog at the start of 2018 I've used numerous sketches for inspiration for paintings and web designs. In addition to sketchs I started recording writing, designs, paintings, and anything else I would scratch down. Overall I'm excited to the collection at the end of the year and to continue to develop the site and provide content every day.
