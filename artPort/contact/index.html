About Nate Walker

A freelance web developer living in Philadelphia with a passion for art. Born in Doylestown PA he moved to Quakertown PA and received his BA in History from Moravian College in 2012. During these years drawing historical characters as cartoons became a passion and would blossom into the expressionist style characters portrayed in his paintings. This summer his art will be on display at various local shops in Philadelphia including the Philadelphia Folk Society. Check out his art blog or his shop at society6
